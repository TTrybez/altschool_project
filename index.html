BIOGRAPHY

Early Life and Dreams

I was born and raised in Gboko, a vibrant town in central Nigeria where dreams often seem distant yet within grasp. Since I was a child, my gaze was often drawn to the skies above and the intricate world of technology. I dreamed of becoming a pilot, soaring through the clouds, or mastering the realm of cyber technology.

Challenges and Determination

Growing up in Gboko wasn't without challenges. Due to some unforeseen crisis which included finances and the untimely demise of my mum, I had to drop out of school temporarily. But despite the setback of loosing my biggest fan and cheerleader i wouldn't bend to defeat. I was curious to fly and this was sparked by the occasional sight of airplanes passing overhead. I spent evenings gazing up at the stars, imagining myself in the cockpit, navigating through the vast expanse of the sky because beyond being my desire, it was my mums as well.

Passion for Technology

My fascination with technology began with being around my elder brohter whom at the time was a tech trainee I salvaged. I would wake up to seeing him focus on his system all night trying to solve issues for people and that stirred me to admiration. Despite his outdated hardware he always came up with solutions to issues sometimes thought unimaginable.

Educational Journey

As I progressed through my education, my determination to pursue my passions only grew stronger. I excelled in mathematics and science, laying a solid foundation for my future endeavors. Recognizing my potential, my teachers and mentors encouraged me to explore my interests further.

Having almost lost hope because of the misfortune of dropping out of my tetiary institution, i was introduced to ALTschool Africa.

Alt School Africa

At Alt School Africa, I found a nurturing environment where my dual interests in technology and aviation could flourish though one above the other. I chose to study backend engineering, diving deep into the complexities of software architecture and data management with the expectation that the rigorous coursework will challenge me, making me to thrive on the intellectual stimulation and practical applications.

Personal Growth and Interests

Outside of my studies, music is a source of solace and inspiration for me. I taught myself to play the guitar and often used music as a means of expressing my emotions and thoughts. Whether strumming chords alone in my room or performing for friends, family and church, music has always been an integral part of my life.

Community Impact

Beyond my personal pursuits, I possessed a deep-seated desire to make a positive impact on my community. I volunteered my time and skills to teach computer literacy classes at local schools, empowering young minds to embrace the digital age. My innate empathy and willingness to help others earned me respect and admiration from my peers and mentors alike.

Future Aspirations

Looking ahead, I envision a future where I can merge my technical expertise with my passion for aviation. Whether I eventually take to the skies as a pilot or pioneer groundbreaking advancements in cyber technology, one thing remains certain: my journey from Gboko to Alt School Africa is just the beginning of an extraordinary adventure fueled by determination, curiosity, and a relentless pursuit of my dreams.

Being a part of AltSchoolAfrica is one journey i do not regret embarking on and you too can make your dreams come true. You can start your journey today by signing up here!
